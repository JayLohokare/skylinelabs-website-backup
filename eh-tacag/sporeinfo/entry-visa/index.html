9th International Conference on Materials for Advanced Technologies

icmat 2017 | 18 - 23 June 2017 | Suntec Singapore

Entry Visa

All visitors to Singapore must have the following:

  • Valid travel document (minimum validity = 6 months at the time of departure);
  • Onward / return ticket;
  • Entry facilities, including visas, to the next destination;
  • Sufficient funds to stay in Singapore and
  • Visa for Singapore (if applicable *)

For other details on entry visa requirements, click HERE to visit the Singapore Immigrations and Checkpoints Authority Website.

We advise participants who require entry visa to contact their local travel agent, or apply for their visas  at least 30 days before the conference.

Application Options

(1) Submission through Immigration & Checkpoints Authority’s (ICA) Online Portal

Processing time is one working day for applications via the Immigration and Checkpoints Authority’s (ICA) online portal Submission of Application for Visa Electronically (SAVE)

Note: Please have your credit card on hand for making payment online.

(2) Submission through Authorized Visa Agents

It is possible to process your visa through an Authorized Visa Agents in your country.

Please Click HERE to access the list of authorized agents.

 

(3) Submission through Trusted Partners

It is highly recommended that foreigners from Assessment Level II Countries apply for their visas through a Singapore-based trusted partner (such as Westminster Travel*). This will help to ensure smooth processing and delivery of your visa application.
