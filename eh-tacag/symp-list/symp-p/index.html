icmat 2017 | 18 - 23 June 2017 | Suntec Singapore

Symposium P

Progress and Challenges in Molecular Electronics

Category: Functional Materials

Christian Albertus NIJHUIS
National University of Singapore, Singapore

Symposium Scope

Proposed Sessions/Topics:

  • Molecule-electrode interface engineering
  • Mechanisms of charge transport
  • Progress in junction fabrication methods
  • Progress in theory and modelling methods
  • Biomolecular electronics
  • Molecular spintronics
  • Thermoelectric effects
  • Molecular plasmonics
  • Understanding long range charge transport
