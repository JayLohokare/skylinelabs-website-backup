Symposium U

Halide Perovskites for Optoelectronics Applications

Category: Optical / Photonics

Symposium Scope

This symposium will focus on the recent advances in the field of organic-inorganic hybrid perovskite materials, and their use as optoelectronic material in e.g. solar cells and light-emitting devices.

  • Perovskite photovoltaics
  • Perovskite LEDs
  • Lasing, light-emitting transistors (LETs), spintronics, etc.
  • Multidimensional perovskites
  • Lead-free perovskites
