About

My passion lies in creating uniquely personal, honest portraits of my subjects. I will work to understand who they are and how they see themselves in the process of choosing how and where we will create their images. I will utilize my experience, technical expertise, and artistic sense to create images with lasting value and impact.

Hi, I'm Bhushan! I'm a Family, wedding, Lifestyle, Couples & Fashion Photographer and I prefer to work with natural light as I find it the most flattering. Sunset and Sunrise are my favorite times to have a photo shoot. I aim to please and strive to capture life and emotion. A session with me will be fun, and overall, a joyful experience. So relax and let your personality shine through! I love taking photos I love capturing you and your family just as they are I love what I do and mostly having fun! Look forward to hearing from you
